Lasagna

Description

The following is the recipe for lasagna. Lasagna is a popular dish enjoyed by many all across the world. This page outlines the steps to preparing lasagna, which is fairly straightforward and easy to follow.

Ingredients

  1. Meat
  2. Onion and garlic
  3. Tomato products
  4. Sugar
  5. Spces and seasoning
  6. Lasagna noodles
  7. Cheeses
  8. Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover the foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
